# Piano sample attribution

The piano recordings used by this site are **Salamander Grand Piano V3** by
Alexander Holm: a Yamaha C5 recorded at 48 kHz / 24-bit with 16 velocity
layers, hammer releases, string resonance and pedal noise.

- Source: https://github.com/sfzinstruments/SalamanderGrandPiano
- Distribution source: https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Piano/acoustic-grand-piano.html
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported
  https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

The deployment workflow converts the lossless FLAC recordings to 48 kHz,
320 kbps MP3 for broad browser support. No lower-quality intermediate source is
used.
